Why I Created This Guide

From MS Diagnosis
to Complete Freedom

In January 2020, I was diagnosed with Multiple Sclerosis at the age of 34 at London Bridge Hospital. The doctors were clear — there was no cure. I left that room despondent and without hope.

What changed everything was a revelation from God's Word. I discovered that Jesus Christ, at Calvary, bore not only my sins but my sicknesses — that healing was not something I needed to beg for, but something He had already purchased for me.

By late spring of 2020, every single symptom was gone. No seizures. No vertigo. No muscle spasms. No blurred vision. Six years later, I remain completely free.

This guide contains the 43 scriptures that formed the foundation of my healing. I took them as medicine — daily, believing that what God said was more powerful than what my body was saying.

They will work for you too. Not because of who you are — but because of who He is, and what He has already done.

"All that the Father gives Me will come to Me, and the one who comes to Me I will by no means cast out."

You can enter covenant relationship with God right now. It is the simplest and most significant thing you will ever do. If you mean it in your heart, pray this prayer:

A Prayer of Salvation

I receive You now as my Lord and Saviour. I believe that from this moment I am saved, healed and made whole by Your finished work at Calvary. Thank You, Lord Jesus. Amen.
